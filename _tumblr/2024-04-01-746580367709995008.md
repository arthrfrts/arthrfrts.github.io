> # April
> 
> by Mary Oliver
> 
> I wanted to speak at length about  
> the happiness of my body and the  
> delight of my mind for it was  
> April, a night, a  
> full moon and –
> 
> but something in myself or maybe  
> from somewhere other said: not too  
> many words, please, in the  
> muddy shallows the
> 
> Frogs are singing.
